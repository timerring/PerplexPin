# Privacy Policy for PerplexPin

**Last updated:** February 27, 2025

## Overview

PerplexPin is a browser extension that pins your preferred AI model on Perplexity.ai. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how PerplexPin handles user data.

## Data Collection

PerplexPin does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. Specifically, we do **not** collect:

- Personally identifiable information
- Health information
- Financial or payment information
- Authentication information
- Personal communications
- Location data
- Web history
- User activity
- Website content

## Data Storage

PerplexPin stores only your selected AI model preference locally on your device using the browser's built-in `localStorage` API. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

## Permissions

PerplexPin requires no special browser permissions beyond displaying a popup when you click the extension icon. The extension does not access or modify any web pages directly through extension APIs.

## Third-Party Services

PerplexPin does **not** use any third-party services, analytics, tracking tools, or external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository: [https://github.com/timerring/PerplexPin](https://github.com/timerring/PerplexPin)
